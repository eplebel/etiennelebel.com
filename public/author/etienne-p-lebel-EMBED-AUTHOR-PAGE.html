Etienne P. LeBel

Independent Meta-Scientist, KU Leuven

I investigate the credibility of scientific research. My current efforts are focused on Curate Science, a unified platform to allow researchers, journals, universities, and funders to ensure that research is transparent (i.e., meets minimum transparency standards) and credible (e.g., survives follow-up scrutiny via new sample replications). I previously lead PsychDisclosure.org, a grassroots initiative that contributed to raising transparency standards at leading journals in psychology. My background is in social psychology where I examined racial biases/prejudice and methodological issues in psychology (e.g., units of measurement standardization; suboptimal research practices that decrease replicability).
